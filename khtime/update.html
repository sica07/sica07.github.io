Update KHTime

How to update:

  • 0. [Optional but recommended] Go to your KHTime folder and backup the current khtime executable (rename it to something like khtime_backup)
  • 1. Download the package for your Operating System
  • 2. Move the downloaded package into your KHTime folder (overwrite the existing package if needed)
  • 3. Enjoy your new version of KHTime
